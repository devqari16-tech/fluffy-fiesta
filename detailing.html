Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full detail take?

Basic detail takes 2-3 hours, Premium detail takes 4-5 hours, and Luxury detail takes 6-8 hours. Time varies based on vehicle size and condition.

What's included in paint correction?

Paint correction removes swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation using professional polishing compounds and techniques to restore your paint's clarity and gloss.

Do you offer mobile detailing?

Yes! We provide mobile detailing service throughout the GTA. We bring all equipment and supplies to your location for convenient service.

How often should I detail my car?

We recommend full detailing every 3-6 months depending on usage. Regular maintenance washes can extend time between details.

What's the difference between wax and ceramic coating?

Wax provides 2-3 months of protection, while ceramic coating lasts 2-5 years with superior durability, gloss, and hydrophobic properties.
