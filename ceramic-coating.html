Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ceramic coating application take?

Basic coating takes 6-8 hours, Premium takes 8-10 hours, and Ultimate protection takes 12-16 hours. This includes paint correction, surface prep, and proper curing time.

What is the difference between coating packages?

Basic offers single-layer protection for 2-3 years. Premium includes multi-layer coating with 5-7 year protection and 9H hardness. Ultimate provides maximum protection with lifetime warranty and additional services.

How should I maintain my ceramic coating?

Regular washing with pH-neutral soap, avoiding automatic car washes, and periodic maintenance sprays will keep your coating performing optimally. We provide detailed care instructions and maintenance kits.

Can ceramic coating be applied to any vehicle?

Yes, ceramic coating can be applied to cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, and boats. The application process may vary based on the vehicle type and paint condition.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer 2-3 year warranty on Basic, 5-7 year warranty on Premium, and lifetime warranty on Ultimate packages covering defects, premature failure, and environmental damage.
