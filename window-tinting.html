Frequently Asked Questions

What are the legal tint limits in Ontario?

In Ontario, front windows (driver and passenger) must allow at least 70% of light to pass through. Rear windows have no restrictions and can be tinted as dark as you prefer.

How long does window tinting take?

Most vehicles take 2-4 hours depending on the package and vehicle type. We'll provide an accurate time estimate when you book your appointment.

What's the difference between ceramic and standard film?

Ceramic film offers superior heat rejection, durability, and clarity compared to standard film. It doesn't fade or bubble and provides better UV protection while maintaining excellent visibility.

Do you offer mobile installation?

Yes! We provide mobile installation service throughout the GTA. We'll come to your location with all necessary equipment for professional installation.

What's included in your warranty?

Our warranty covers bubbling, peeling, discoloration, and adhesion failure. Premium packages include lifetime warranty with free replacement if any issues occur.
