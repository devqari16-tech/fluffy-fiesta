Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save on energy costs?

Most customers see 20-30% reduction in cooling costs during summer months. The exact savings depend on your building size, orientation, and current HVAC system efficiency.

How long does commercial installation take?

Installation time varies by project size. Small offices take 1-2 days, while large commercial buildings may take 1-2 weeks. We work around your business hours to minimize disruption.

Will window film affect natural light?

Our advanced films are designed to block heat and UV rays while maintaining excellent visibility and natural light. You'll notice reduced glare but not reduced brightness.

Do you offer financing for large projects?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for commercial projects over $5,000. Contact us to discuss financing options that work for your budget.

What's the return on investment?

Most commercial and residential projects pay for themselves within 2-3 years through energy savings. Combined with increased comfort and property value, the ROI is excellent.
